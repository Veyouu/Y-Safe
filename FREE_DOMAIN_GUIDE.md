## Google Search (SEO) - Can People Find It?

**YES! Your site CAN appear in Google search results!**

### How to Get Your Site Indexed in Google:

#### Step 1: Deploy Your Site
- Use platform subdomain (best option)
- Make sure site is **public** (not private)

#### Step 2: Add SEO Meta Tags
Your site already has SEO meta tags added:
- Title tags
- Description meta tag
- Keywords
- Proper meta tags for search engines

#### Step 3: Submit to Google Search Console
1. Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Sign in with Google account
3. Click "Add Property"
4. Enter your URL: `https://y-safe.up.railway.app`
5. Verify ownership:
   - Choose "HTML file" method (easiest)
   - Download verification file
   - Add to your public folder
   - Upload and verify
6. Click "Sitemaps"
7. Submit: `https://y-safe.up.railway.app/sitemap.xml`

#### Step 4: Wait for Indexing
- Google crawls new sites in 1-3 days
- Can check status in Search Console
- Submit sitemap speeds up process

#### Step 5: Share Your Site
- Share on Facebook, Twitter, etc.
- Get backlinks from other sites
- Google finds sites faster when shared
