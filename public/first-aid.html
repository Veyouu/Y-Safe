First Aid Tutorial

First aid is the immediate care given to a person who is injured or suddenly ill before professional medical help arrives. Knowing basic first aid skills can help prevent injuries from getting worse, reduce pain, and even save lives. This tutorial will guide you through essential first aid techniques using a triangular bandage, including its parts, proper folds, and correct knot tying. You will also learn how to manage common injuries such as wounds on the head and forearm, burns on the hand, proper use of an arm sling, and care for burns on the face or back of the head. By learning these basic skills, you become more prepared to respond calmly and confidently in emergency situations.
